1993 Mini Edition

Gameplay Aditions

Cosmetic Changes

By the early 90s, more detail was added to the bottom edge of the scene. Most likely so the scene could be used in merchandising with different aspect ratios. Some of the added detail is visible in this edition, though most is cut off by the page boundary.

1997 Special Edition

Gameplay Additions

  • Wizard Whitebeard, Odlaw, and the mystery character were added
  • The key, camera, bone, scroll, and binoculars were added
  • Waldo and the bag were moved to new locations
  • 2 Waldo Watchers were added

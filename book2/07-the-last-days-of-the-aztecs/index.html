History Book

1988

The Aztecs lived in sunny Mexico and were rich and strong and liked swinging from poles pretending to be eagles. They also liked making human sacrifices to their gods, so it was best to agree with everything they said. The Spanish were also rich and strong and some of them, called conquistadores, came to Mexico to find gold. They thought the Aztecs a complete nuisance. Get swinging and find me in Mexico.

2014

The Aztecs lived in sunny Mexico and were rich and strong and liked swinging from poles, pretending to be eagles. They also liked making human sacrifices to their gods, so it was best to agree with everything they said. The Spanish were also rich and strong, and some of them, called conquistadors, came to Mexico in 1519 to have an adventure. However, when the Aztecs and Spanish met, they did not agree on much.
